Project Overview

In this project, i look into the thriving sector of online retail by analyzing a transactional dataset available at the UCI Machine Learning Repository. The sole goal of this project is to bring light to the efficiency of marketing strategies and boost sales through customer segmentation from the information in the customer data.

Libraries Used

  • Scikit-Learn
  • Surprise Library
  • sklearn.cluster
  • Matplotlib/Seaborn
  • warnings
  • linregress from scipy.stats
  • plotly.graph_objects
  • tabulate
