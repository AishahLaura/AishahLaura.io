Employee Data Analysis

The great resignation decoder, predicting attrition before it happens. How data can save your best employees

Project Overview

Employee attrition is a silent killer of productivity and growth. High turnover leads to lost knowledge, recruitment costs, and team morale decline. But what if we could predict attrition before it happens and take action?

This project leverages HR analytics and machine learning to uncover why employees leave and how companies can retain top talent. By analyzing employee data, we identify key risk factorsâ€”helping businesses turn insights into action and protect their most valuable employees.
